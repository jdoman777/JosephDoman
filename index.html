FamilySearch Advertising Campaign

FamilySearch is an incredible brand, but they aren't the most appealing company to Gen Z. This was the point of my internship: market effectively to BYU students. After considering FamilySearch's brand position and Gen Z's tendencies, I created a fleshed out advertising campaign for FamilySearch. I defined the ideal target market on BYU campus, outlined specific marketing goals and strategies to best reach the target market, and built mockups of various creative executions.

This advertising campaign provided FamilySearch with relevant insights about Gen Z and a potential way to reach these students. Understanding Gen Z will help FamilySearch level up their marketing efforts.

Frazil Partnerships

Interns actually can make a real-world difference

Frazil is the fastest growing slush company in the world. Because of that growth, they're looking for anywhere and everywhere to promote their brand.

This is where my internship came in. I was assigned two different projects to boost Frazil's brand. The first was to recruit over 60 independently-owned chains for the Free Frazil Friday (FFF) campaign, which I did with a 63% success rate. I also coordinated the delivery of hundreds of cases of Frazil and marketing materials to the recruited locations.

My second project was to create and present an official partnership proposal to Frazil's executive board. I conducted dozens of hours of secondary research to determine which companies are most brand compatible with and strategically attractive to Frazil. I narrowed all this research down to 3 companies that would best play to Frazil's strengths. You can see my full presentation at the link below.

Ultimately, the executive board chose to implement one of my suggestions: they partnered with the Utah Hockey Club, the newest professional sports team in Utah and the hottest team in the entire NHL. This partnership made brand awareness explode, as almost 500K people watched fans guzzle Frazil for a chance to win a year's supply of Frazil in this past season alone. It went so well that they are renewing the partnership for this upcoming year.
